## Problem Description
Job seekers lose opportunities not because they were rejected — but because they forgot to follow up. Managing recruiters, referrals, and networking contacts across scattered emails and spreadsheets is chaotic and unreliable. Loopify solves this by acting as a personal memory and timing system: it tracks every contact, logs every interaction, and sends automated daily digest emails so no follow-up ever slips through the cracks.
